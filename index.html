Calling & Theology

I am committed to a Christ-centered theology that emphasizes the transformative power of the Gospel. My convictions are rooted in the authority of Scripture and the mission of the local church.

"I believe that the call to preach is a call to prepare. This is why I am currently pursuing theological certificate programs alongside my active ministry. Transitioning from a background in Physics to the study of God’s Word requires a new set of tools—linguistic, historical, and hermeneutical. These programs are not just about earning certificates; they are about stewardship. By sititng under seasoned scholars, I am ensuring that the 'Faith Beyond Walls' I preach is grounded in the historic Christian faith and communicated with the clarity that today’s world demands."

Statement of Faith

God: Eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
Creation & Logic: God is the author of both the Word and the World; there is no conflict between true science and faithful scripture.
Salvation: By grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.
The Church: The body of Christ, commissioned to make disciples and impact society.
